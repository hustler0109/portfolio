Egg Catcher Game

Project information

Category: Python & Game

Experience the thrill of catching falling eggs in this interactive Egg Catcher game. Control the basket using keyboard keys or mouse movement, and aim to catch as many eggs as possible while avoiding the dropping ones. With dynamic speed levels, lives to manage, and a competitive scoring system, Egg Catcher offers engaging gameplay for players of all ages. Showcase your quick reflexes and strategic skills in this fun and addictive game!!
